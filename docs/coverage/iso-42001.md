## Boundary disclaimer

HUMMBL is **not** an ISO 42001 certification body. Certification requires an accredited registrar conducting Stage 1 + Stage 2 audits. ISO 42001 is an **AI Management System (AIMS)** standard — it requires an organizational system for managing AI risks across the lifecycle, similar in structure to ISO 27001 ISMS. HUMMBL contributes the technical evidence substrate; the AIMS itself is the customer organization's responsibility.

## Structure

ISO 42001:2023 follows the Annex SL high-level structure used across ISO management system standards:

- **Clauses 4–10** — AIMS requirements (Context, Leadership, Planning, Support, Operation, Performance evaluation, Improvement)
- **Annex A** — Reference controls (~38 controls organized into 8 control objectives)
- **Annex B** — Implementation guidance for Annex A controls
- **Annex C** — AI-system-specific objectives and risk sources

## Clauses 4–10 — narrative

ISO 42001 Clauses 4–10 require the customer organization to establish, implement, maintain, and continually improve an AIMS. HUMMBL contributes:

- **Clause 7.5 (Documented information)** — same primitive as ISO 27001 Clause 7.5: append-only governance bus + cognition ledger
- **Clause 8 (Operation)** — AI risk + impact assessments stored as tuples; AI lifecycle traced via tuple chain
- **Clause 9 (Performance evaluation)** — continuous monitoring via governance bus; internal-audit query primitives
- **Clause 10 (Improvement)** — nonconformity + corrective-action tracking

The AIMS itself — leadership commitment, AI policy authorship, organizational scope — is the customer organization's responsibility.
