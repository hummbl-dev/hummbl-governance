# HUMMBL Governance Security Policy

## Supported Policies

- All policy documents maintained in this repository are versioned under v1.1 or higher.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

- Submit via email: [security@hummbl.io](mailto:security@hummbl.io)
- Provide description, affected policy/artifact, and suggested mitigation.
- Expect initial acknowledgment within 3 business days.
